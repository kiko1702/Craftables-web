About CraftAbles


CraftAbles is a business that handcrafts things and sell them. It was
owned by Selim Khaled and Mark Samer. CraftAbles was
founded by Mark Samer and Freddy Magdy.

It all started when Mark and Freddy were together in "Sahel" and decided
to look through old handcrafted goods. They found beads and strings
and decided to make a ring (CraftAbles ring).
It looked so good that they wanted to sell a few of it on the beach.
Unfortunately they didn't have time to make enough rings to sell.
Then when they went back to their homes, Mark showed his sister the
ring he handcrafted. Then Mark got an idea to start a business that sells
everything that they can handcraft.
He told Selim later that day in his training and he liked the idea so much.

They started giving each other ideas, working on products and the
website. Then Selim wanted to quit, because he thought he didn't have enough
creatvity to run such a business.

Then Mark replaced Selim with Freddy Magdy. Who was been a huge
help. He made bracelets and suggested lots of ideas.
Freddy also owns 50% of CraftAbles (Mark owns the other 50%) runs the
TikTok page, tests the website and makes these beautiful crochets you
see everywhere on our website.
